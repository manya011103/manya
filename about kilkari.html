About Kilkari

Kilkari is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of underprivileged children through access to education and healthcare. Our mission is to create a world where every child, regardless of their economic background, has the opportunity to thrive.

Our Mission

To provide essential medical care and educational opportunities to poor children who otherwise cannot afford them. We believe that every child deserves a chance to live a healthy, educated, and empowered life.



Our Vision

A future where every child can grow up in a supportive environment with the tools they need to build a better life for themselves and their communities.



Our Core Values

Compassion: We care deeply about the children we serve and are committed to their well-being.
Transparency: We maintain transparency in all our operations and fund management.
Community: We work together with local communities to bring about sustainable change.
